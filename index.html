Welcome to EcoCraft

EcoCraft is a social enterprise that creates handmade products from recycled materials while providing job opportunities for disadvantaged communities.

By turning waste into useful products, we help protect the environment and support sustainable living.

About Us

EcoCraft focuses on transforming recycled materials into creative handmade products. Our mission is to reduce environmental waste while creating job opportunities for people who face difficulties finding stable employment.

Through creativity and sustainability, EcoCraft aims to build a greener future and inspire people to live more environmentally friendly lifestyles.

Our Products

Recycled Tote Bags made from reused fabrics.

Handmade decorations created from recycled materials.

Eco-friendly accessories designed for sustainable living.

Reflection

Creating this website helped me understand how social enterprises can solve both environmental and social problems at the same time.

Through researching and designing EcoCraft, I learned that businesses can generate profit while also helping communities and protecting the environment. This project showed me that sustainability and innovation can work together to create positive change.
