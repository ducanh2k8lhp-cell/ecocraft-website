Welcome to EcoCraft

EcoCraft is a social enterprise that creates handmade products from recycled materials while providing job opportunities for disadvantaged communities.

By turning waste into useful products, EcoCraft helps reduce environmental pollution and encourages sustainable living.

About EcoCraft

EcoCraft focuses on transforming recycled materials into creative handmade products. Our mission is to reduce environmental waste while providing employment opportunities for people who struggle to find stable jobs.

Through innovation and sustainability, EcoCraft hopes to inspire people to protect the environment and support ethical businesses.

Our Products

Recycled tote bags made from reused fabrics.

Handmade decorations created from recycled materials.

Eco-friendly accessories designed for sustainable living.

Reflection

Creating this website helped me understand how social enterprises can solve both environmental and social problems at the same time.

After learning about social enterprises, I realized that businesses can generate profit while also helping communities and protecting the environment.

This project inspired me to think about how creativity and sustainability can work together to create positive change in society.
